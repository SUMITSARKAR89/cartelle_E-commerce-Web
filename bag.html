    Caring for Your Leather Bag

    Caring for Your Leather Bag

    — Isabelle R.

    Event Planner

    Mar 4, 2025 - 7 min read

    A high-quality leather bag is an investment, and with proper care, it can last for years. Here’s how to keep your leather looking as good as new.

    Regular Cleaning for Lasting Shine

    Dust and dirt can wear down leather over time. Wipe your bag with a soft, dry cloth regularly to remove buildup. For deeper cleaning, use a leather-specific cleaner to maintain its natural texture and color.

    Protecting Against Wear & Tear

    Leather is sensitive to moisture and sunlight. Avoid prolonged exposure to direct sunlight, as it can cause fading and drying. Keep your bag away from excessive heat and humidity to prevent cracking.

    Storing Your Bag the Right Way

    When not in use, store your bag in a dust bag or a soft pillowcase to maintain its shape. Avoid stacking heavy items on top of it, and stuff the interior with tissue paper to preserve its structure.

    With these simple steps, your leather bag will remain a timeless accessory that you can enjoy for years to come.
